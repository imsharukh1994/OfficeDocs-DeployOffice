# Overview of Microsoft 365 Companions People app (preview)

The People companion app is designed to enhance team collaboration and productivity by providing quick access to employee profiles, organizational charts, and contact information. This app can be pinned to the taskbar for easy access, allowing users to find colleagues, understand team structures, and connect with the right people for any task or project.

:::image type="content" source="media/people-app.png" alt-text="Windows desktop showing the People app open, displaying a list of favorite and top contacts with availability status and communication options.":::

## Key features

- View a person’s card – Quickly access detailed profiles of colleagues, including their roles, contact information, and more.
- Availability information – Check the availability status of team members to find the best time to connect.
- Org chart navigation – Explore organizational structures to understand team hierarchies and reporting lines.
- Start a conversation – Initiate chats or emails directly through Microsoft Teams or Outlook for seamless collaboration.

## Availability

Currently, the People companion app is only available for versions of Microsoft 365 that include Teams.
